About

I’m a passionate game developer with a strong focus on programming and a deep appreciation for game art. I began coding at the age of 15, starting with simple HTML and CSS out of pure curiosity and a desire to learn. Since then, I’ve expanded my skill set to include scripting in Lua for FiveM, as well as developing games in Godot using GDScript and in Unity with C#. Currently, I’m diving into Unreal Engine, learning both Blueprints and C++.

My main strength lies in game programming—whether it’s building core mechanics, optimizing performance, or crafting gameplay systems—but I also have a creative side. I’ve been passionate about graffiti since I was young, and that love for visual expression continues to inspire the artistic aspects of my work.

I'm 20 years old and currently in my second year of the Videogames and Multimedia Applications (VAM) program at Universidade Lusófona. Alongside my studies, I’m currently working at a company that supplies refurbished electronic devices, where I’m responsible for handling and managing RMAs (Return Merchandise Authorizations).

My goal is to create games that are not only technically solid but also capable of delivering meaningful and memorable experiences to players.
